About Me

I am an industrial automation, robotics, and IT professional with over a decade of experience developing effective solutions for machine control and automation. I'm proficient in several programming languages including C, C# and assembly. I'm also skilled in analyzing and reverse engineering communication protocols, debugging and optimizing embedded firmware, and creating robust, reliable, and safe machine control software and hardware. I have direct experience with many different platforms and technologies in the automation industry, ranging from very old and otherwise obsolete to the latest and greatest. Regardless of what equipment you have or what your requirements are, I can develop a solution to provide the desired machine functionality.
